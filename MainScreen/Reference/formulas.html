Ohm's Law
Voltage (V)
V = I × R
Current (I)
I = V / R
Resistance (R)
R = V / I
Power
Electrical Power (P)
P = V × I
Power (with Resistance)
P = I² × R = V² / R
Components Series & Parallel
Resistors in Series
Req = R₁ + R₂ + ...
Resistors in Parallel
1/Req = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...
Capacitors in Series
1/Ceq = 1/C₁ + 1/C₂ + ...
Capacitors in Parallel
Ceq = C₁ + C₂ + ...
